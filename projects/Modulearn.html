ModuLearn

A modular lesson site, putting an end to the rabbit hole of prerequisite searching.

What is it?

Often times, a problem may arise where you may need to find a quick solution beyond your skill level. Conventionally, this can mean long periods of research, wandering down an endless hole of partial solutions, or often solutions that assume knowledge you might not posses.

Modulearn is intended to be a platform not unlike other solution and community driven education platforms in which users may add their own experise and insight on a given topic. However, Modulearn is set apart from this as it is designed to not only provide you with the high level solution, but also all prerequisite information needed to better understand the material, and cover any and all gaps in knowledge.

Importantly, there is no need to rewrite what others have already posted! Contributing is as easy as writing a new section, and linking to an existing tutorial chain.

How to use it

After searching for solution to your problem, you may also specify your level of experience, and adjust as necessary.

Are you an expert looking for a quick reference? Modulear will only supply you with the most concise answer.

Are you a novice looking to tackle a challenge? Depending on how confident you are, or even if you want a review, anything down to the most basic help will be provided in a section-by-section page, each building on the other.
